# Service Level Agreement (SLA)
**PT Semen Nusantara Web Platform**

## 1. Service Description
This SLA covers the PT Semen Nusantara Enterprise Web Platform, including:
- Public website (semen-nusantara.com)
- Admin portal for contact submissions
- Contact form processing
- Analytics and reporting dashboards

## 2. Availability Guarantee
- **Uptime SLA**: 99.5% per month
- **Excluded Downtime**: Scheduled maintenance (max 4 hours/month, announced 7 days prior)
- **Measurement**: Monitored from multiple global locations

## 3. Support Tiers

### Tier 1: Premium (Recommended)
- **Response Time**: 4 hours (critical), 24 hours (standard)
- **Resolution Time**: 24 hours (critical), 5 business days (standard)
- **Cost**: $2,000/month
- **Includes**: 
  - Priority queue
  - Dedicated support contact
  - 24/7 emergency hotline
  - Monthly business review

### Tier 2: Standard
- **Response Time**: 8 hours (all issues)
- **Resolution Time**: 5 business days
- **Cost**: $500/month
- **Includes**: Email/Slack support, business hours only

### Tier 3: Community
- **Response Time**: Best effort (3-5 business days)
- **Cost**: Free
- **Includes**: GitHub issues, community forum

## 4. Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Page Load (LCP) | < 2.5s |
| First Input Delay | < 100ms |
| Cumulative Layout Shift | < 0.1 |
| Form Submit Latency | < 1s |
| Admin API Response | < 500ms (p95) |

## 5. Security & Compliance

- **Data Encryption**: TLS 1.3 (in-transit), AES-256 (at-rest)
- **Backups**: Daily automated backups, 30-day retention
- **Audit Logs**: 12-month retention
- **Compliance**: 
  - GDPR (EU only)
  - POPIA (South Africa)
  - ISO 27001 (pending certification)

## 6. Data Protection & Privacy

- **Personal Data**: Encrypted and accessible only to admins with 2FA
- **Retention**: 2 years for contact submissions (configurable)
- **GDPR Right to Deletion**: Honored within 30 days
- **Data Subjects Rights**: Accessible via admin portal

## 7. Disaster Recovery

- **RTO** (Recovery Time Objective): < 4 hours
- **RPO** (Recovery Point Objective): < 1 hour
- **Backup Location**: Geographically distributed
- **Failover**: Automatic to backup region

## 8. Credits & Remedies

If uptime falls below SLA:

| Uptime | Monthly Credit |
|--------|----------------|
| 99.0% - 99.4% | 10% |
| 98.0% - 98.9% | 25% |
| < 98% | 50% |

Credits applied to next month's invoice. Maximum aggregated credits: 50% per billing period.

## 9. Limitations

Not covered by SLA:
- Customer-caused outages or misconfigurations
- DDoS attacks (unless Shield enabled)
- Third-party service failures (e.g., Supabase)
- Emergency patches required within 24 hours

## 10. Service Changes & Termination

- **Pricing Changes**: 30 days notice
- **Feature Deprecation**: 90 days notice
- **Account Termination**: Either party with 30 days notice
- **Data Retention**: 60 days after termination
